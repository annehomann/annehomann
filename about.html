About Me

I am a passionate designer and web developer who has an avid interest in technology and making websites beautiful. Aside from having my Bachelor in Graphic Design, I have complemented this by completing a course in UX which increased my interest in the psychology of human behaviour related to user experience.

With experience in front end technologies such as HTML/CSS,
Javascript and jQuery and also back end technologies such as
Ruby on Rails, I am always strengthening these skills with practice
and working on my own projects.

Currently, I am undertaking the Cyber Security bootcamp with Coder Academy and have had the opportunity to learn Python which is exciting to add to the skill set. I am looking forward to completing this course and getting started in a career in Cyber Security.

Outside of technology, my interests are music, sport, photography and trying new gins.
